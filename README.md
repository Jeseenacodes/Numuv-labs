## ✨ About Numuv Labs

**Numuv Labs** is an independent product studio focused on creating AI-powered solutions that make learning, personal growth, and decision-making more accessible.

Our work spans:

* 📚 Educational Technology
* 🤖 AI-Powered Applications
* 📊 Data Analytics & Business Intelligence
* ⚡ Automation & AI Workflows
* 🎯 Product Strategy & Design

Every product is built with a human-centered approach, combining data-driven insights with thoughtful user experiences.

## 🚀 Featured Products

### 📖 Little Noories

An AI-powered early literacy platform designed to help children develop reading confidence through engaging stories, interactive learning, and educational experiences.

### 🌍 Tamilingo

A language learning platform helping Tamil diaspora children strengthen their language skills while staying connected to their cultural heritage.

A women's wellness platform focused on supporting wellbeing through thoughtful digital experiences, guided resources, and personal growth.
