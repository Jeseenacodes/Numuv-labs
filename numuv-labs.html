Founder Story

to AI Product Studio

A journey shaped by a genuine belief that the right tools can transform how people learn, grow, and make decisions and a conviction that educators make the best product builders.

Chapter One
The Educator

Nearly nine years in education teaching, curriculum design, and learning experience development. Built a deep intuition for how people absorb information and what makes something genuinely useful.

Chapter Two
The Analyst

Moved into analytics with Power BI, DAX, SQL, and data modeling. Earned certifications from Microsoft and IBM. Built 15+ dashboards across healthcare, telecom, retail, and finance with measurable outcomes.

Discovered the intersection of AI and product design. Built an EdTech AI Operations Suite across n8n, Make, Relay.app, and Zapier. Shipped three live products and a suite of AI assistants.

Mission

Building Technology That Makes Learning More Human

My mission through Numuv Labs is to create meaningful AI-powered experiences that help people learn, grow, make better decisions, and unlock opportunities that were previously out of reach.

I believe technology should amplify human potential not replace it. And I believe the people who've spent years in classrooms make the most empathetic, effective product builders.

  • ✦ AI should serve people, not the other way around
  • ✦ The best products are built from genuine lived understanding
  • ✦ Children and women deserve tools built with them in mind
  • ✦ Data without context is just noise analytics should tell stories
